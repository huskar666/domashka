Dota 2

The main goal in the game Dota 2 is to destroy the enemy - a huge building on the enemy base in its center, reminiscent of a magical citadel, while simultaneously defending against attempts by the enemy team to do the same. If you destroy the throne before the opposing team, you will win.

Dota 2 wiki








Dota 2 is played by 2 teams of 5 people. Both teams have their own base in opposite corners of the map and defend it during the game. Each of the ten players independently controls a powerful character called a hero[6], each with unique abilities and a different play style. The team that first destroys the central building of the enemy base wins, for this the heroes during the game try to kill the enemy heroes and supporting creatures (creeps) and destroy the defensive enemy buildings, thus gaining experience and gold for their development. If the hero dies, he is reborn after some time.
